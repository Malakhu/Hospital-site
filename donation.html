Benefits of blood donation

  • Reduce stress
  • Improve your emotional well-being
  • Benefit your physical health
  • Help get rid of negative feelings
  • Provide a sense of belonging and reduce isolation

Rules for donors

  • To donate blood or platelets, you must be in good general health
  • You must weigh at least 110 pounds
  • You must be at least 16 years old
  • Parental consent is required for blood donation by 16 year olds, no parental consent is required for those who are at least 17 years old.
